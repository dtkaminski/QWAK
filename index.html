FAQs

What makes Qwak different?

We’re not a bank, budgeting app or comparison site. Qwak is your AI life-admin agent — capturing your bills, organising your contracts, and switching your utilities when better deals appear.

What can Qwak help with?

Energy, broadband, insurance, phone, car, subscriptions — plus all the everyday admin like receipts, warranties and renewals.

Do I have to upload everything manually?

No. Qwak auto-captures your bills and contracts, so you don’t have to forward or file things yourself.

Will Qwak ever switch me without permission?

Never. Qwak only acts when you approve. You’re always in control.

Do you compare the whole market?

We work with trusted providers and update rates regularly. Our methodology is transparent and we explain which providers we include.

How do you keep my data safe?

Bank-grade encryption, GDPR compliance and full transparency. You can download or delete your data anytime.

How does Qwak make money?

We disclose any commission we earn, and we always show you the best option first. Our incentives are aligned with yours.

Can I leave anytime?

Yes — there are no lock-ins. If Qwak isn’t working for you, you can walk away instantly and take your data with you.

What’s the catch?

There isn’t one. You get less admin, more time, and real savings — that’s it.
